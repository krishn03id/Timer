Why HIIT?

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is the most potent trigger for BDNF (Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor). Unlike steady cardio, the "spike" in heart rate signals the brain to prepare for survival by upgrading neural hardware.

Physiological Benefits

  • Mitochondrial Biogenesis: Creates new power plants in cells.
  • Lactate Shuttle: Uses metabolic waste as super-fuel for the brain.
  • Neuroplasticity: Increases synaptic density in the Hippocampus (Memory).
